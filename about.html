Empowering Progress Through Energy Excellence

Discover SATORP’s journey, values, and commitment to driving sustainable innovation, global partnerships, and national transformation.

SATORP is one of the world’s leading integrated energy and petrochemical companies, driven by innovation, strategic partnerships, and a commitment to operational excellence. We create value across the hydrocarbon chain—delivering reliable, efficient, and high-quality products that serve vital industries and communities around the globe.

We envision a future where energy powers sustainable growth and transforms lives. Our ambition is to be a global leader in energy transition—offering reliable, affordable, and more-sustainable energy while helping shape a net-zero carbon economy through advanced technologies and responsible practices.

Our mission is to generate long-term value for our stakeholders by maintaining the highest standards of safety, performance, and environmental stewardship. We are committed to empowering our people, supporting our partners, and positively impacting society while addressing the world’s evolving energy challenges.

Our Shareholders

SATORP is the result of a powerful partnership between saudi aramco and totalenergies, combining world-class expertise in refining and petrochemicals, strong financial backing, and a long-term commitment to industrial excellence. this collaboration represents a shared legacy of success and innovation in energy, with both companies playing a vital role in shaping the future of sustainable energy development.

Saudi Aramco

A global energy leader and one of the world’s largest integrated oil and gas companies. Saudi Aramco plays a key role in the global energy transition, delivering reliable, sustainable energy while creating long-term value for communities and shareholders.

TotalEnergies

An international multi-energy company active in over 130 countries, TotalEnergies is committed to producing more affordable, cleaner, and accessible energy. With a legacy of innovation, it leads the way in building a responsible energy future.
