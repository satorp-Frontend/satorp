Internal Citizenship

We integrate Health, Safety, Security, and Environment into every aspect of our operations, protecting people, assets, and the environment. Through strong systems and a culture of responsibility, we ensure safety, performance, and sustainability at every level.

External Citizenship

SATORP launched the External Citizenship Activities Program to support local communities and stakeholders through outreach initiatives such as philanthropy, awareness campaigns, and traffic safety programs. As part of our CSR strategy, we established Torathuna, a company dedicated to empowering artisans and preserving Saudi heritage through sustainable craft entrepreneurship. In collaboration with the Saudi Commission for Tourism and National Heritage, Torathuna provides funding, training, and tools to help local craftsmen build thriving businesses. We also partnered with Wa’ed Ventures to strengthen support for the handicraft sector and ensure long-term impact through national collaboration.
