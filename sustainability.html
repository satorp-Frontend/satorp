About Sustainability at SATORP

Discover how sustainability drives our strategy, operations, and long-term impact, aligning with Vision 2030 and global environmental goals.

Our Commitment to a
Sustainable Future

At SATORP, sustainability is embedded in every aspect of our operations. We aim to minimize our environmental footprint, conserve resources, and foster innovation that supports a low-carbon future. Our strategy is aligned with global sustainability goals and the Kingdom’s Vision 2030.

Prioritizing Safety,
Securing Excellence

We uphold the highest HSSE standards to protect our people, our partners, and the environment. Through robust safety protocols, continuous training, and proactive risk management, we ensure a secure, healthy, and compliant workplace that empowers everyone to work confidently and responsibly.

View HSSE Policy

Environment and Health

We enable SATORP to achieve a world-class safety and environmental culture while safeguarding company premises. By integrating Health, Safety, Security, and Environment into daily operations, we enhance company performance, save lives, and protect assets.

Our goal is to excel in environmental and health standards by adhering to all regulations, minimizing impacts, implementing effective waste management, raising awareness, and actively promoting sustainable practices that protect the environment and the well-being of our employees and contractors.

SATORP’s Golden Safety Rules

To foster a culture of safety and accountability, SATORP has defined nine Golden Safety Rules that every employee and contractor must follow. These rules are designed to prevent incidents, promote awareness, and ensure a consistent standard of safe behavior across all operations. By adhering to these principles, we collectively uphold our commitment to safety excellence.

Personal Protective Equipment

Minimize exposure hazards that cause serious injuries or illnesses through proper use of PPE.

Management of Change

Ensure all modifications are reviewed and approved before implementation to control risks.

Citizenship
Empowering communities through purpose-driven initiatives.

Internal Citizenship

We foster a supportive and engaging environment for our employees and their families through initiatives like health programs, safety awareness, and environmental workshops.

External Citizenship

Citizenship Continuity

Volunteering

Our Social Responsibility Strategy

Guided by values. Committed to lasting impact.

At SATORP, our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) strategy is more than a commitment — it's embedded in the way we operate. Aligned with both national priorities and global sustainability goals, our approach focuses on creating long-term value for people and the planet.

External Citizenship

We invest in strategic initiatives that promote environmental stewardship through conservation and sustainability programs, advance education by empowering future generations with learning opportunities, support economic development by enabling local industries and entrepreneurs, and foster community empowerment through inclusive outreach and social initiatives. CSR at SATORP is not a separate function — it is a foundational mindset that guides every aspect of our operations.
