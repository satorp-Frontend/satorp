Our Business

Driving value through innovation, technology, and strategic collaboration.

Business Units
at a Glance

SATORP operates through a network of specialized business units that collaboratively manage the refinery’s full value chain—from feedstock intake and processing to product delivery and global export. Each unit plays a key role in ensuring operational excellence, safety, sustainability, and long-term economic contribution.

Operations

Manages refinery processes, controls production units, and ensures quality standards are met.

Maintenance & Reliability

Ensures continuous operations by maintaining equipment health and addressing potential risks.

Technical Services

Oversees process optimization, lab analysis, and technical troubleshooting.

Innovating for a
Smarter Future

At SATORP, technology is not an option—it’s the foundation of our success. We integrate cutting-edge digital systems, process automation, and predictive analytics to optimize production, improve safety, and reduce emissions.

From Raw Inputs to
Refined Excellence.

SATORP transforms heavy crude oil into high-value refined products using advanced technologies and best-in-class operational systems. Our operations are designed to maximize yield, ensure safety, and minimize environmental impact.

LPG

Used as fuel for heating, cooking, and industrial processes with lower emissions.

Propylene

Used in the production of plastics, textiles, packaging, and automotive components.

Gasoline

Refined to meet international standards, powering vehicles with performance and efficiency.
